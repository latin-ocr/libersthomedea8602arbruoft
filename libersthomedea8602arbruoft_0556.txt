456 REGISTRUM DE ABERBROTHOC.

Doloquhy · Soluendo sexaginta et quatuor libras monete Scoeie ad festa
Pentheeostes et Sanai Petri ad uincula infra monasterium de Arbroyth ·
de quaquidem summa abbas concedit prefato pro suo fideli seruieio et
consilio quatuor libras annuatim omnibus diebus vite sue · 28 Augusti

1526.

Eadem affedaeio Scotiee in hunc effthum—Be it kend ws Dauid
abbot of Arbroyth to haue set to Allexander Ogylwy of that ylk
hys ayris and affignais the teynd schauys and frutis of our kyrkys of
Banf and Innerbondy wythin the dioey of Aberdon for nynten yeris ·
quhylkis the sayd Allexander had in tak of ws of befoyr · Payand tharfor
yeirly to ws wythin the brweh of Aberdeyn the sowm of thresehoyr fowyr
pundys vsuall mone of Seotland at the fest of Paehe and Lammes · And
has to the sayd sowm of fowr pundys atowr the sayd threscoyr of pundys
yeyrly we dischargis the sayd Allexander tharof for steyd profeit and
plesar down to ws · And we sall warrand to the sayd Allexander the tak
of the faydis teynd sehawys aganys all dedle beeaws we haue resauit fra
the sayd Allexander sowmys of mone and profeyt befoyrhand · 28 August
1526.

Abbas affedat pro nouendeeim annis J aeobo Douglas filio Dauid Douglas ·
J onete Ewgstone eius matri · Arthuro Douglas Jacobi filio · eorumque
affignatis et subtenentibus non maioris status terras de Tullequhylle et
Mallemuk eum decimis garbalibus de Tullequhylle in baronia de Ban-
quhoryterne et regalitate de Arbroyth · quasquidem terras et decimas
dietus Dauid prius in affedacione habuit · Soluendo nouem libras et
decem solidos monete Seocie eum rynmart wedder et aliis seruiciis et
oneribus husbandorum · 28 Augusti 1526.
