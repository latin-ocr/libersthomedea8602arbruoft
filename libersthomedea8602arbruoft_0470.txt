370 REGISTRUM DE ABERBROTHOC.

[Indentura de battella portus de Athy]

This indenture maid at Abbirbroth the aucht day of the moneth of
September the yeir of Gode ane thousande fywe hundreth and sex yeris

betuix ws George . . . abbot of . . . Abbirbroth . . . ande Thomas
lorde of Innermeith ande baron of Innerkelor . . . beiris vitnes . . .
as eftyr folowis . . . We George abbot forsaid with the consent of owr

conuent for hartly lufe we hawe to the saide lorde Thomas of owr fre wil
gefis licens to the saide Thomas lorde to bryng ane fische boit in oure
hawyn of Athy for al the dayis of his lywe · and his fischaris to pas and
repas thairto with their stuf and geir fik as ganis tham ande to lay thair
anchoris apon lande · to wis hyng ande dry nettis · to tak fische · ande
all othir necefl'ar thingis to do vse exers and hant neidful to fisching craft
ande sawyng of the boit and geir · quhilk vse sal induce na pofl'effione to
the saide lorde of Innermeith nothir grunde rycht nor seruitute bot sal be
alwais without preiudice or hurt til ws owr conuent ande place of Abbir· Α
broth and owre success'ouris eftir the dede of the saide Thomas lorde · In
witnes heirof to a part of this indenture to ramane with ws . . . the
seille of Thomas lorde forsaid is 'appendit · ande to the part . . . to
ramane with the said Thomas lorde we hayf appendit the commone selle
of our ehaptour . . . befor thir witnes - mastyr Hew Dowglace dene of
Brechin · Alexander Hepburn of Quhitfim · Adam Hepburn of the Crage
knycht · Aleiander Guthre of that ilk · J ames Haye 'of N achtane
knyeht · Robert Gray sone to lord Gray · John Ogilwy of the Crage ·

mastixfi Dauid Carale ande mastir James Farchar notairis · with othiris
diuers.
